A State of Trance podcast RSS feed.

If you’re looking for the RSS feed of Armin Van Buuren’s “A State of Trance” podcast, the proper link is:

http://www3.armadamusic.com/podcasts/podcast.xml

You can add this to Google Reader, and then view and download all past episodes directly from Google.

You can also find raw media files for episodes 146 and later (as of 1/4/2011) via URLs like this:

http://podcast.armadamusic.com/podcasts/audio/episodeXXX.m4a

You’ll have to transcode to mp3 format yourself if that’s what you want.
