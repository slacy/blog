MyTob.BT update

Sort of interesting things have been happening. I’ve noticed that the virus e-mails slow down during the day to only 2-4 per hour, and pick up at night to 10-15 per hour. In the last 24 hours, I’ve recieved 198 messages containing Worm.MyTob.BT or one of its variants.

Almost all of them are addressed to “gallery — slacy.com” which is an e-mail address that got published in a couple places:

1. The gallery-devel mailing list
2. The gallery online docs (contributor)

So, its the combination of being in many people’s address books (via the mailing list) and accessible via the web thats the double whammy for virus e-mails.
