Sharing the fun: Instructables

Instructables has lots of innovative and fun looking projects.

I particularly liked the LED Throwies & Twisted Spoke Bicycle Wheel Lacing

I find Instructables cool because of the variety of projects. From hardcore electronics, to burning-man style bikes, to Pesto Egg Crepes, all on one site. Its fun just reloading the home page, which always shows 3 random projects. I’m also intrigued by the flickr-style image stuff they’re using. Its cool.
