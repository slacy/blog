“Fearless” on OLN

One of the great shows to premier this season (thus far) is “Fearless” on OLN. It’s a series of 1-hour documentaries about people who have come through extraordinary circumstances. The 3 episodes that I’ve seen so far have been about a base jumper, a girl who got bitten by a shark, and a woman who spent 6 weeks alone at sea after her ship was nearly destroyed in a hurricane. Its full of inspiring stories from people who have survived the worst, and come out better. Set your TiVos.
