PCB123 works under Wine


Well, I finally had success getting the PCB123 installer to run, and after that, the program actually seems to work!  I haven’t done any real design using it yet, or ordered any boards, but I will in good time, just be patient.  The secret is getting the latest Wine, and making sure you force the installer into fullscreen mode, by doing something like this in your \~/.wine/config file:

; PCB123 installer
[AppDefaults\pcb123.exe\Version]
“Windows” = “win2k”
[AppDefaults\pcb123.exe\x11drv]
“Desktop” = “800x600”

That should just about do it.  There was a special DLL that was missing (a vbscript runtime thing?) but I google’d for it, found it on MS’s site, downloaded the installer, and it worked just fine after that.  Good luck!
