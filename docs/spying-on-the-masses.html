Spying on the masses…

So, I got this Eye-Fi card from work, and it works okay. The interesting thing is that when it posts photos to the internet, it adds the tag “Eye-Fi” to all the photos. That means you can search on flickr, smugmug, photobucket, picasaweb, and a variety of other photo hosting sites to see whats happening right now! Remember, Eye-Fi uploads instantly to the web, so these are the absolutely newest photos!
