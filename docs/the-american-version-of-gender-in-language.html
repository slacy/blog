The American version of “gender” in language.

Many romance languages have a gender for each noun. But, we don’t have this in English, and I always wonder what its like to just “know” what gender some random object should be.

Never fear! In American English, I realized that for most food-nouns, we have something nearly equivalent to gender: Foil vs. Plastic Wrap!

Take a look at this list…

Brownies: Foil
Rice Crispie Treats: Plastic Wrap

Pizza: Foil
Cheese: Plastic Wrap

Cupcakes: Foil
Frosting: Plastic Wrap

Bread: Foil
Sandwiches: Plastic Wrap

Cooked Hamburgers: Foil
Raw Hamburger: Plastic Wrap

I’m sure there are more…
