meevee — pointless?

Okay, so driving back from San Francisco this weekend, I saw a giant billboard for meevee near the airport. I went to the site to check it out, and entered my zipcode and TV provider. I found the following:

  • It pegs the CPU of my 2.4 GHz machine and freezes all my browser windows for 10-30 seconds for every page load. I presume this is because its laying out all 400+ channels of DirecTV into one giant table. Nice moove, guys. Time to learn AJAX.
  • Its nothing more than TV listings with advertisements.
  • Even the mildly entertaining features like “Most Popular” are based on Neilsen data, instead of the user-supplied ratings. Blah.
  • I like the way it looks, its just horridly slow.
  • Why would I ever use this if I have TiVo?
  • “Interactive” flash-based ads in the corner are mildly interesting, mildly annoying
