Upgrading Slimserver to 5.4 broke my Squeezebox (and then I fixed it)

Just in case anyone else has this problem, here’s the deal:

If you’re running Sveasoft’s firmware on your Linksys WRT54G router, and you upgrade to the latest version of the SlimServer from slimdevices.com, your Squeezebox may stop working.  The problem is that for some reason, the squeezebox and the slim server seem to all think their IP addresses are 192.168.1.1 (the IP of the router itself).  This was being caused by the setting “loopback” under the Administration tab.  When I turned that off, it worked fine.  That said, I have no idea why I had turned on Loopback in the first place.  Maybe it was the default?
