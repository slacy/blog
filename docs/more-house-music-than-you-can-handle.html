More house music than you can handle.

If you’re looking for house/dance music, you can get some from Beats In Space.  They’ve got gigs and gigs of downloads, just start mining at beatsinspace.net/audio.  These are recordings from a show on WNYU, so most mp3s are continuous mix, approximately 1 hour long, usualy in 2 parts.  Voted a “Best Of…” by the Village Voice. I’m still debating if I want to download and import these into my music collection.  A 1-hour long dance mix really throws a wrench in a cool random mix.
