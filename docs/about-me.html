About Me

Hi, I’m Steve, nice to meet you. {.alignright .size-medium .wp-image-1542 width=”300” height=”300”}

I’m a Software Engineer living in the heart of Silicon Valley.  I worked at Google from 2005 to 2010, and for the majority of that time, I was the Tech Lead of the Video Streaming & Storage team.  I also spent time working on the Storage Infrastructure team, and on the Ads Quality team.  You can see more at my LinkedIn profile.

Prior to Google, I worked at TiVo doing full-stack development, mostly focusing on middleware and architecture for Pioneer and DIRECTV products.

I attended Carnegie Mellon University for my undergraduate degree in Computer Science, and have been living here in Silicon Valley since 1994.

Today, I’m an entrepreneur working on Proovn.  My goal is to radically improve hiring & recruiting for Software Engineers.

You can contact me via LinkedIn, Twitter, or Quora, and I’ll try my best to get back to you in a timely manner.  Thanks for visiting!
