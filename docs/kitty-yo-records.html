Kitty Yo Records


Todays music download comes courtesy of Kitty-Yo records.  Despite the pink, orange and blue website, and the “kitty” name that might make you think its Japanese.  Its not, its a german independent label.  I found it through some Maximillian Hecker that I had in my collection from a previous download session, and found out that kitty-yo is his label, and that there’s a bunch of stuff to download.  I’m not sure what form most of this stuff takes (i.e. what the heck its like) because I’m just downloading it now and haven’t sampled it yet.  I’ll update when I get a better feel for it.  But, if Maximilian Hecker is any example, this stuff is melodic, downtempo, sensitive, and pretty cool.  Have fun.
