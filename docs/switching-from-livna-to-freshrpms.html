Switching from Livna to Freshrpms

For my off-distro needs (xmms-mp3, mplayer, etc.) I always had a hard time deciding between dag, livna and freshrpms.

Unfortunately, configuring a single machine to pull from all 3 of the above repositories is basically impossible (since they export slightly different builds of the same versions of a bunch of packages, like mplayer).

Today, I’ve switched from Livna to Freshrpms for 3 reasons:

1. Firefox 1.5 for x86_64 is in freshrpms and not livna.
2. ???
3. profit!
