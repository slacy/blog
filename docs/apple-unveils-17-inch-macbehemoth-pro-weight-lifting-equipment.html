Apple unveils 17-inch MacBehemoth Pro weight lifting equipment

AppleInsider | Apple unveils 17-inch MacBook Pro with 8-hour battery.

Or, more appropriately titled:  Apple flips the bird to the concept of a ‘netbook’.

Apple’s new laptop:

Is 3 times heavier than the average netbook.

Has 3.75 times more pixels onscreen than a netbook.

Has 3.5 times the CPU of a netbook (single 1.6GHz Atom vs. Dual 2.93GHz)

Has a screen that’s almost 2 times larger than an 8.9” netbook.

Has 4 times as much RAM as a netbook.

Has 2 times as much disk space as a netbook.

Lasts the same amount of time on battery as a 6-cell netbook. (i.e. Acer Aspire One)

Oh, and did I mention that it costs about EIGHT TIMES as much as an average netbook?
