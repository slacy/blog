A little GPS data to keep you happy.

Yeah, been recording a lot of stuff with the GPS, and I figured people might be mildly interested in taking a look at it.

I’m still coming up with a good software display solution (using Google Maps) but until thats done, its easy to export these things into Google Earth as KML files. So, with no further adieu, I present you… our drive from Bear Valley, CA to Mountain View, CA

(Note: you’ll need to install Google Earth from http://earth.google.com before being able to really view that link. In fact, you may need to “Save As” and then open the .kml file directly from Earth)
