Roxy Re-modeled

I came across this great album called Roxy Re-modeled on EMusic the other day. Its a whole album of downtempo covers of all the best Roxy Music songs. Slave To Love, Avalon, Angel Eyes, etc. Starting at BasicLUX.com, there are lots of good links to more downtempo music stores, (dogsonacid, beatport, newsoundtheory) but I haven’t been able to find a good place for some free scrapes of this sort of stuff yet.
