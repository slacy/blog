Yes, it is.

MetaFilter posed this question: Is the Kleptones “A Night at the Hip-Hopera” better than DJ Danger Mouse’s “Grey Album“?

Well, I’m happy to say that yes, it is much much much much better. I was very underwhelmed by the Grey Album. It was interesting, but just a mish-mash that I didn’t really find that appealing. The Kleptones on the other hand have made something really special. Lots of pop-culture references (The Mooninites!), and a really cool mix of HipHop, Eminem, and Queen, of all things. Check it out, while you still can.
